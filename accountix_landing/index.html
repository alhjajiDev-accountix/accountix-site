Description

Accountix is a powerful and practical accounting application designed to simplify money management for small businesses and individuals. This offline-first app ensures all your financial data, including accounts, transactions, and records, is securely stored locally on your device, giving you complete control and privacy.

With a lot of features:

  • Comprehensive Money Management: Track income, expenses, and transfers with ease.
  • Account & Fund Management: Create and manage various accounts (cash, bank, personal, business, etc.) and funds, including setting default options.
  • Intuitive Interface: Designed for a seamless and efficient user experience.
