Privacy

Privacy Policy for Accountix Last updated: June 20, 2025

Thank you for using Accountix. Your privacy is important to us.

  1. Overview Accountix is a personal and small business accounting app that operates completely offline. The app does not collect, store, or share any personal data with us or any third party.

  2. Permissions We Request To provide core functionality, Accountix requests access to:

  • Contacts: This is used only to simplify the process of adding accounts by pre-filling fields with contact information. The app does not collect or transmit contact data.
  • Storage: Used solely for saving local backups and restoring your data when needed.

These permissions are used strictly on-device and never leave your phone.

  1. Data Handling
  • All data is stored locally on your device.
  • You have full control over your data: you can edit, delete, or back up your entries at any time.
  • Since the app is fully offline, you are solely responsible for maintaining and protecting your locally stored information.

  1. Third-Party Services Accountix does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or cloud storage services.

  2. Children's Privacy This app is not directed at children under the age of 13. No data is knowingly collected from any user.

  3. Changes to This Policy If any changes are made to this Privacy Policy in the future, we will update this page. Please review it periodically.
